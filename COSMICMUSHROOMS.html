COSMIC MUSHROOMS

A physics-based 3D isometric space game where you control a rocket and navigate through levels with environmental hazards and enemy units. The game features 3 different worlds, 24 handcrafted levels, and offers at least 1.5 hours of gameplay. Gameplay is focused on careful movement, gravity-based physics, and precise control. The game is inspired by classic gravity-based games such as Crazy Gravity.
